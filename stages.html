Modern Methods

  • Hydroponics: Hydroponics is a soil-less method of growing plants using a water-based nutrient solution that provides all the essential minerals and nutrients for plant growth.
  • Vertical Farming: Vertical farming is an innovative agricultural method of growing crops in vertically stacked layers, often within specially controlled indoor environments. This soilless technique conserves land and water resources by increasing yield per unit area.
  • Smart Irrigation: Smart irrigation is a technology-driven method that uses sensors, weather data, and automation to deliver the right amount of water at the right time, optimizing plant health and conserving resources.
